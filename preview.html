OPUS Silk Chocolate is Volka Food's flagship premium chocolate product, launched in 2021 as part of the luxury OPUS range. The bar features a smooth milk chocolate shell with a silky interior ganache, made from sustainably sourced cocoa. It is designed for both retail shelf placement and premium gifting — available in retail-ready individual bars, branded gift boxes, and bulk export trays.
For trade buyers and distributors: OPUS Silk Chocolate is available with trade pricing for verified distributors and retail chains. Minimum order quantities start from 6 cartons (150g SKU). Volume pricing available from 50+ cartons. Net 30 payment terms available for established accounts. Contact our sales team at trade@volkafood.com or use the trade enquiry form above for a formal quote within 24 hours.
